Rajma Chawal Recipe

Description

Rajma Chawal is a classic North Indian dish that combines the rich flavors of kidney beans with aromatic spices, served over steamed basmati rice. This wholesome and hearty meal is a staple in many households, loved for its comforting taste and nutritional value. The tender kidney beans are cooked in a flavorful onion-tomato gravy, infused with a blend of spices, creating a deliciously robust curry. Paired with fluffy basmati rice, Rajma Chawal is a satisfying and wholesome dish that brings warmth to your table.

Ingredients

  • cup Rajma (kidney beans), soaked overnight
  • 1 cup Basmati rice, washed and soaked for 30 minutes
  • 2 large onions, finely chopped
  • 3 tomatoes, pureed
  • tablespoons oil or ghee
  • teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon red chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • coriander leaves for garnish and Kasuri methi (optinal)

Steps

  1. Boil the soaked rajma in a pressure cooker until tender. Set aside.
  2. Heat oil or ghee in a pot, add cumin seeds, and let them splutter.
  3. Sauté chopped onions until golden brown.
  4. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and chopped green chili. Sauté until the raw aroma disappears.
  5. Add tomato puree, turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Cook until the oil separates.
  6. Mix in the boiled rajma and simmer for 15-20 minutes to meld flavors.
  7. Cook soaked basmati rice until fluffy.
  8. Serve rajma over steamed basmati rice, sprinkle garam masala, and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  9. Enjoy your homemade Rajma Chawal, a comforting North Indian delicacy!
